Career Goals

I have a science-heavy background in terms of my education because I took many Rutgers health care classes that my school provided. My previous plans for college were to study to become a pharmacist. Unfortunately, I struggled with chemistry and calculus during my first semester at NJIT. This made me realize that there was a low chance of developing a successful career in pharmacy and I needed to shift my life in a different direction. After a bit of online research and discussions with others, I decided to switch my major from B.S. Biochemistry to B.S. Web and Information Systems. I am particularly interested in learning more about UX, and although I don't have much of a background in coding or web development, I'm very willing to learn more about it. I hope to develop a clearer goal of what new career I want to pursue as I gain more experience from the classes and internships.
